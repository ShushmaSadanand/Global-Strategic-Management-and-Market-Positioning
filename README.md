## Project: Strategic Management & Global Expansion Risks (Xiaomi)
**File:** `Strategic Management of xiaomi.pdf` (Executive Analysis Slide Deck)

A comprehensive corporate strategy case study analyzing the global growth trajectory, competitive ecosystem advantages, and long-term vulnerabilities of the world's third-largest smartphone brand, **Xiaomi**.

### Core Business & Strategy Insights
* **Global Market Distribution Mapping:** Evaluated the stark contrasts in Xiaomi's international market penetration. Analyzed the drivers behind its massive regional dominance in emerging territories (e.g., controlling 44.3% of the mobile market in neighboring Kyrgyzstan, 36.2% in Myanmar) alongside its strategic stagnation in major Western markets.
* **Deconstructing Market Entry Barriers:** Analyzed the strict socio-political and legal friction points that limited Xiaomi's scale inside the United States (holding just a 0.7% market share), specifically diagnosing network carrier restrictions and regulatory containment policies (such as the historical Department of Defense restrictions).
* **Supply Chain & Geopolitical Risk Modeling:** Formulated a rigorous risk-and-mitigation framework addressing Xiaomi’s reliance on highly centralized global production networks. Outlined the tactical necessity of establishing regional manufacturing hubs across Asia, Europe, and North America to shield against sudden geopolitical trade disruptions.
* **Premium Repositioning Strategy:** Outlined clear, cross-functional, actionable steps to overcome limitations in luxury brand perception, leveraging targeted high-end marketing campaigns and local brick-and-mortar retail partnerships to scale beyond low-margin consumer segments.
